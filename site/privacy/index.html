Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

📦

Data we collect

Promptory collects the prompts you send to AI tools, along with the AI's response and basic metadata (platform, timestamp). Your data stays on your device unless you explicitly opt in to sharing.

🌐

Community & sharing

The Community feature is strictly opt-in. If you choose to participate, prompts are automatically screened to remove personal information before being shared. You can withdraw at any time from within the extension.

🤝

Third parties

If you opt in, anonymized, de-identified data may be shared with or sold to third parties to improve our services. Your personal identity is never attached to any data we share. If you don't opt in, your data never leaves your device.

⚙️

Your controls

You can export your data as JSON or CSV anytime, or delete everything with "Clear all". Uninstalling the extension removes all your data. Community consent can be withdrawn from the Community tab at any time.

✉️

Contact

Questions? Reach us at info@promptory.chat. We may update this policy as the product evolves — changes will be reflected by the date above.
