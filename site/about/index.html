About

Built for people who take AI seriously.

Promptory started as a personal frustration — and turned into the tool we wished existed.

If you use AI tools every day, you know the feeling: you write a brilliant prompt, get a great result, close the tab — and then spend the next week trying to remember exactly how you phrased it. You rewrite it from scratch. It's never quite as good.

That's the problem Promptory solves. It runs quietly in the background, capturing every prompt you send across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Grok, and Copilot. No copy-pasting. No manual saving. No workflow changes. Just open the side panel when you need something, and it's there.

We built Promptory with one non-negotiable: your data stays on your device. We were frustrated with tools that hoovered up your data to train models or power dashboards we didn't care about. Promptory stores everything in your browser's local storage. We don't have a server receiving your prompts. We can't read them. They're yours.

What we believe

🔒

Privacy is not a feature

Local-first storage isn't a marketing claim — it's a technical constraint we chose. We couldn't collect your data if we wanted to.

⚡

Zero friction

Tools that require manual effort don't get used. Promptory is invisible until you need it — then it's instantly there.

🎯

Do one thing well

Promptory saves, organizes, and surfaces your prompts. We won't bloat it with AI features, prompt marketplaces, or social feeds.

🌐

Platform agnostic

We don't pick favorites between AI tools. Promptory works wherever you work — across all major AI platforms, equally.

The roadmap

Promptory v1 is focused on doing the core job reliably. What's next:

  • Folder organization — group prompts into projects or workflows
  • Browser sync — optional encrypted sync across your own devices
  • Community library — browse high-quality prompts shared by the community (opt-in)
  • Firefox support
