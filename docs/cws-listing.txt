══════════════════════════════════════════════
 WHY THE COMMA-LIST HAD TO GO ENTIRELY
══════════════════════════════════════════════

CWS's automated keyword-density detector apparently scores the
specific PATTERN "BrandA, BrandB, BrandC, BrandD, BrandE, and
BrandF" highly regardless of how many other words surround it.
This is a known anti-keyword-stuffing heuristic — listing
competitor brands in commercial copy is the textbook spam pattern
they're filtering for, even when the listing is factually accurate.

Workaround: communicate multi-platform support through:
  - Title (currently mentions ChatGPT specifically)
  - Screenshots (show the side panel listing prompts from
    multiple platforms — visual proof, no keyword density)
  - Promo tile (lists platform names as logos/dots, not text)
  - Privacy policy / website (more latitude there)

Don't enumerate platforms in the store description copy.
